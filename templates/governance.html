S-RAIEF Framework

Security: Protecting AI systems from misuse and data leaks.

Accountability: Ensuring decisions are traceable and owned.

Trust: Building confidence through transparent design.

Explainability: Making model reasoning visible and clear.

Fairness: Guarding against bias and ensuring equity.

S-RAIEF forms the foundation of my AI Governance approach — balancing innovation with integrity.
